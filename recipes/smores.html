Warm S'mores

Description

S'mores are a classic and beloved treat that combines the goodness of melted marshmallows, rich chocolate, and crisp graham crackers. It's a delightful combination of flavors and textures that evokes feelings of warmth and nostalgia. Often enjoyed during outdoor gatherings, campfires, or cozy evenings, s'mores offer a satisfying and indulgent dessert experience. The combination of gooey marshmallow and melted chocolate sandwiched between two crunchy graham crackers creates a mouthwatering sensation that is hard to resist. Whether enjoyed by oneself or shared with friends and family, s'mores are a simple yet irresistible treat that never fails to bring joy and smiles to those who partake in their deliciousness.

Ingredients

  • Graham crackers
  • Marshmallows
  • Chocolate bars

Steps

  1. The marshmallow is gently toasted over an open flame until it becomes gooey and slightly golden.
  2. Once done, it's placed on top of a square of chocolate, which begins to melt from the residual heat.
  3. The marshmallow and chocolate are sandwiched between two graham crackers, creating a delightful combination of sweetness, creaminess, and crunch.
